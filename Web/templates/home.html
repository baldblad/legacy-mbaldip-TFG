This web is the culmination for the Final Project of a Data Science Degree by Martí Baldi. The abstract for the project is found below:

Abstract

This project addresses the development of a website that uses data collected from Twitter to fulfill two purposes: 1) ease the understanding and raise awareness about the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and 2) monitorize the actions of representative organizations or individuals with a strong relationship with the SDGs. The process to achieve this consists of the following. First, the training of a binary classifier that, based on the tweet’s text, determines whether a tweet is related to the SDGs or not. Second, a multiclass document classifier that is trained to label tweets into one of the 17 SDGs. Finally, a graph analysis based on tweet references (retweets, replies and quotes) to determine the organizations and individuals mentioned in purpose number 2. Our results show that it is, indeed, possible to build a tool that matches such conditions.

Keywords: Twitter, SDGs, Global Goals, document classification, graph analysis, web development.
